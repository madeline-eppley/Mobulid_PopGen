### Relatedness
Genotype data were converted from VCF to GDS format using SNPRelate (1.32.2; [Zheng et al. 2012](https://academic.oup.com/bioinformatics/article/28/24/3326/245844?login=false)). We estimated pairwise genomic relatedness on only biallelic SNPs using the KING method of moment estimator implemented in `snpgdsIBDKING()`. This method computes a kinship coefficient for every sample pair, estimating proportion of genome shared identical-by-descent (IBD) under a moment-based framework.  Resulting kinship coefficients were compiled into a symmetric relatedness matrix, which was visualized as a heatmap with Euclidean distance hierarchical clustering to clarify patterns of genetic similarity among individuals.
